About Us

This is what we do.

The Kulmayers were established on March 18th, 2023. That's right. We are just a couple of newlyweds trying to create a beautiful life with what we have and are so excited to share this journey with the world! It's not easy being newly married and cultivating the life you want immediately. But we know it's possible; and if we can make things a little easier for someone else along the way, we are committed to making it happen.

So who are we? Well, there's Dylan, and there's Abigail. Dylan is the techonologically inclined, music-directing-video-editing pro. Abigail loves all things homemaking and beautifying (while simplifying) life. Together, we make a great team and we are committed to creating a family life that is immersed in love, joy, and peace.

We hope to bring value to your life as you endeavor to enrich your life and your relationships. Anything homemaking, food, or garden related, this is the place to be!
