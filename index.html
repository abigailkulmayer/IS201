Easiest Houseplants to Grow in UT

  1. Pothos
    • Water once every few weeks
    • Filtered sunlight
    • Repot once a year
    • Beautiful hanging plant

  2. Jade
    • Water about once a week
    • Filtered sunlight
    • Repot every few years; enjoys having crammed roots
    • Continues to grow for years

  3. String of Pearls
    • Water once every few weeks
    • Filtered sunlight
    • Repot every few years
    • Beautiful hanging plant
